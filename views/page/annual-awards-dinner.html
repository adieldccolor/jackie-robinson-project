The Jackie Robinson Foundation will celebrate its Annual Awards Dinner on Monday, March 9, 2015 at New York’s venerable Waldorf Astoria hotel. The iconic event will pay tribute to individuals who embody the humanitarian ideals of Jackie Robinson and raise funds for the Jackie Robinson Foundation.

The Foundation provides four year college scholarships and extensive mentoring to academically distinguished minority students with financial need and records of leadership capacity. This year, the Foundation is excited to honor Gerald L. Hassell, Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Bank of NY Mellon and Donald Thompson, President and CEO, McDonald’s Corporation. Additional honorees will be announced at a later date.
