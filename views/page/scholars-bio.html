2014-2015 Accomplishments
Daril is now the Program Coordinator for Howard University’s chapter of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers (ASME). He also hopes to lead a mentorship program that will increase his school’s retention rate. Daril researched nano-materials with a Professor from Howard University at the Air Force Research Lab.

Career Aspirations
Currently Daril is unsure exactly what he wants to do once he finishes his under graduate degree, but he hopes to attain a graduate degree in Business or International Relations.

Community Service
Daril takes part in a mentorship program at Maya Angelou Charter School through Simba; Swahili for young lion. Simba is a mentoring program that matches African American male youth with African American men. Daril mentors inner city youths in hopes to influence them to make positive life choices.
